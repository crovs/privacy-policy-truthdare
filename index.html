üîç Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start the game?

Select a category, add player names, choose a game mode, and hit "Let's Play". Truths or Dares will appear for each player's turn.

What are the different game modes?

Classic Mode: Players choose between truth or dare when it's their turn.
Spin the Wheel Mode: The app randomly decides if a player gets a truth or dare question.
Mystery Mode: Special challenges that combine truth and dare elements for an unpredictable experience.

Are there family-friendly options?

Yes! We offer family-friendly categories suitable for all ages. Adult content is clearly labeled and locked behind age verification.

How do I unlock premium content?

Premium categories and features can be unlocked through in-app purchases. Simply tap on a locked category, and you'll see purchase options.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID account settings:
1. Open the Settings app on your iOS device
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Find "Truth or Dare ‚Äì Spicy Party" and tap it
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

üì± Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Check if your iOS is up to date
4. Reinstall the app if problems persist

Purchase not showing up

If you've made a purchase but content remains locked:
1. Tap "Restore Purchases" in the app settings
2. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase
3. Check your internet connection

Content not appropriate

Our app contains a mix of family-friendly and adult-oriented content. You can:
1. Filter content by selecting appropriate categories
2. Enable Family Mode in settings to restrict adult content
3. Report any inappropriate content using the contact form below
