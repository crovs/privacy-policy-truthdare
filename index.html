❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I start the game?
    Select a category, add player names, and hit “Start Game”. Truths or Dares will appear one by one.
  • What is Mystery Mode?
    It’s a surprise dare — you won’t know what it is until you say yes. Refuse it? You get a juicy truth instead. 🎭
  • Is the app family-friendly?
    Yes! There are Family & Kids categories. You can also hide spicy or adult content using Family Mode in Settings.
